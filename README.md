# PagePals

PagePals is a website for people who love reading or people who should probably read more.

The goal of PagePals is to make reading more social, motivating, and fun. Users can log the books they are currently reading or have finished, write reviews and comments, and share their progress with friends.

PagePals helps you stay accountable, discover new books through your friends, and keep track of your personal reading journey.

---

## Features

- **User Accounts And Login**  
  Create your own profile and securely log in to access your personal book log.

- **Book Tracking**  
  Add books you are reading or have already finished, including the date and status.

- **Reviews And Comments**  
  Write short reviews or thoughts about each book to remember what you liked (or didn’t).

- **Reading Tracker with friends**
  Create a "competion" with your friends where you log your reading and a quick summary of what youve read. 

- **Social Sharing with Friends**  
  Connect with friends and share your reading activity to stay motivated together.

- **AI Book Summaries**  
  Get an AI-generated summary of your books if you need help remembering key details.

- **Personal Reading History**  
  Keep a clear overview of everything you’ve read and your progress over time.

---

PagePals is built to encourage consistent reading habits while creating a supportive community of readers.
